Design Reference from Dribbble - SaaS Pricing Page Concept by Abu Raihan

COLOR PALETTE:
- #0C0833 - Very dark blue/purple (main background)
- #323D9A - Deep blue (secondary)
- #5B62B2 - Medium purple-blue (accents)
- #67B2E7 - Light blue (highlights)
- #9DCDEE - Soft cyan (glass effects)
- #94A2D6 - Lavender (subtle accents)
- #E4F4FC - Very light blue (text on dark)

DESIGN CHARACTERISTICS:
1. Dark background (#0C0833) - very deep blue/purple, almost black
2. Glass-morphism cards with subtle transparency and blur
3. Gradient overlays on cards (blue to purple)
4. Clean white text on dark backgrounds
5. Rounded corners on all cards
6. Subtle shadows and glows
7. Purple/blue accent buttons
8. Clear visual hierarchy
9. Pricing cards with glass effect and colored borders
10. Navigation with transparent background

STYLE NOTES:
- Modern SaaS/fintech aesthetic
- Minimalist but sophisticated
- Glass cards have subtle white/light borders
- Featured card (middle) has stronger glow/highlight
- Smooth gradients, no harsh contrasts
- Professional and premium feel

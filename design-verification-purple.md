## Color Palette Used
- Primary Purple: #a855f7
- Violet: #8b5cf6
- Fuchsia: #d946ef
- Background: #08080f
- Card Background: rgba(18, 18, 30, 0.85)
- Border: rgba(139, 92, 246, 0.15)

## Animation Effects
- Card hover: translateY(-6px) scale(1.02)
- Button hover: scale(1.1) with glow shadow
- Smooth transitions: 0.3s cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)

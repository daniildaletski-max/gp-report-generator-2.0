GP Portal Design Analysis
=========================

Current Design Elements:
- Dark gradient background (slate-900 to blue-900 to indigo-900)
- Glass-morphism cards with backdrop blur
- Good color scheme with blue/purple/green accents
- Stats cards with icons and glow effects
- Mobile-responsive layout

Areas for Improvement:
1. Add more visual interest with animated elements
2. Improve the empty state designs
3. Add a welcome/greeting section with personalization
4. Better visual hierarchy for the bonus section
5. Add progress indicators and achievements
6. Improve error details section styling
7. Add subtle animations on hover/load
8. Better typography hierarchy
9. Add a performance score ring/gauge
10. Improve the evaluation history cards

Design Enhancements to Implement:
- Animated gradient background
- Floating particles or subtle animation
- Score gauge/ring for overall performance
- Achievement badges section
- Better card hover effects
- Improved empty states with illustrations
- Animated progress bars

# GP Card Design Notes

## Current Status
The GP cards now show dark glassmorphism styling with:
- Dark blue/purple background with blur effect
- Proper border colors based on attitude status (green for positive, red for negative)
- Cyan/blue text for GP names
- Proper dark theme colors throughout

## Verified Working
- Summary stat cards with glassmorphism effect
- GP individual cards with dark backgrounds
- Attitude buttons with proper dark theme colors
- Footer stats with proper contrast

## Design System
- Background: rgba(20, 20, 70, 0.5) with blur
- Border: rgba(100, 120, 200, 0.25)
- Text: #67B2E7 for names, #9DCDEE for secondary
- Positive: green-400/500 variants
- Negative: red-400/500 variants
